📖 About Info Snap

AI-Powered Document Summarization Made Easy

🚀 What is Info Snap?

Info Snap is an advanced AI-powered document summarization tool that helps you extract key information from PDFs and images in seconds. Whether you're a student, researcher, or professional, Info Snap saves you time by providing concise summaries of lengthy documents.

✨ Key Features

📄

Multiple Formats

Support for PDF, PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, and BMP files

🤖

AI-Powered

Advanced algorithms for accurate text extraction and summarization

🌐

Multi-Language

Translate summaries into 15+ languages instantly

📥

Download & Share

Export summaries as PDF or share via link

🎯 How It Works

  1. Upload your document (PDF or Image)
  2. Choose summary length (Short, Medium, or Long)
  3. Click "Generate Summary"
  4. Get instant summary with key points
  5. Translate, download, or share your summary

💡 Use Cases

📚 Students

Quickly summarize research papers, textbooks, and study materials

💼 Professionals

Extract key points from reports, contracts, and business documents

🔬 Researchers

Review multiple papers efficiently and identify relevant information

📰 Content Creators

Get quick insights from articles and create content summaries

🔒 Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All uploaded files are processed securely and automatically deleted after processing. We don't store your documents or summaries unless you explicitly choose to share them.
